The Journey of Excellence: Excel Football Academy

      Founded in 2017, Excel Football Academy began as a dream—a vision to inspire young talent and transform raw passion into remarkable skill. It was born from a deep love for the game of football and the desire to give back to the community, providing young athletes with opportunities that many could only dream of.

      In a small field with group of dedicated coaches and volunteers gathered, united by one goal: to shape the next generation of football stars. Armed with limited resources but boundless determination, they created a space where young players could not only refine their skills but also learn the values of discipline, teamwork, and perseverance.

      In 2021, Excel Football Academy celebrated its first milestone when one of its alumni was scouted for a professional league—a moment that marked the realization of the academy's potential.

     As Excel Football Academy looks toward the future, it carries with it the same spirit that ignited its beginning in 2017—a vision of excellence, a dedication to the game, and a promise to shape not just players but leaders and role models for generations to come.

Our Goals

        Excel Football Academy exists to positively impact the development of Youths by molding young talents into professional football players with very good character and passion through intensive mental, physical, social and leadership training.

Aspirations

        We envision a future where every passionate footballer, regardless of background, has the opportunity to shine on the global stage. Through dedication, innovation, and community support, we aim to be a leading academy that shapes not only exceptional players but also responsible leaders and role models.

Why We Excel ?

        At Excel Football Academy, we combine world-class coaching, personalized mentorship, and character development to empower our players to excel both on and off the field. By fostering a disciplined, team-oriented environment and creating equal opportunities for all, we ensure every athlete is equipped to achieve greatness and leave a lasting impact on the world of football.

Driven by Purpose

        At Excel Football Academy, our purpose is rooted in the belief that every aspiring footballer deserves a platform to shine. We are dedicated to shaping not only exceptional athletes but also individuals of character and resilience. Through passion, innovation, and an unwavering commitment to excellence, we inspire our players to pursue their dreams and contribute meaningfully to the sport and their communities

Frequently Asked Questions

Get the Game Plan: Your FAQs Answered

What is the ideal age to enroll my child in Excell Football Academy?

     At Excell Football Academy, we believe in nurturing talent from a young age. We typically accept children from 7 years old, as this allows us to lay a strong foundation of skills and knowledge of the game. However, we also offer beginner programs for younger players who show an interest in football, ensuring every child has the opportunity to develop at their own pace.

How can I identify if Excell Football Academy is the right fit for my child?

     We understand how important it is to choose the right academy for your child’s growth. Here’s why Excell Football Academy stands out:

1. Experienced Coaching Staff: Our coaches are not only highly qualified but also have extensive experience in developing young talent. We are committed to fostering each player's potential.

2. State-of-the-Art Facilities: Excell Football Academy is equipped with modern training grounds and top-tier equipment to ensure every training session is productive and enjoyable.

3.Success Stories: We take pride in the achievements of our alumni, many of whom have gone on to play professionally or represent Nigeria at various levels. Our track record speaks for itself!

What is the cost of enrolling my child in Excell Football Academy?

       At Excell Football Academy, we offer competitive pricing to make quality football education accessible. While costs can vary depending on the program, we are committed to offering value that exceeds expectations. We also offer flexible payment plans and scholarships for promising young talents. Please contact us directly to discuss tuition fees and available options.

What should my child expect during training sessions at Excell Football Academy?

       Training at Excell Football Academy is designed to be both challenging and rewarding. Your child will experience:

1. Skill Development: We focus on refining technical skills like dribbling, passing, and shooting. Our training is tailored to each player’s ability.

2. Physical Fitness: Our sessions include exercises to improve strength, endurance, and agility, helping players stay fit and perform at their best.

3. Tactical Training: We teach players game strategies, positioning, and teamwork, ensuring they understand how to read and influence the game.

4. Mental Conditioning: We emphasize resilience and confidence-building, preparing players mentally for the challenges they will face on and off the field.

How can I support my child’s development at Excell Football Academy? consequat?

      At Excell Football Academy, we view the parent-coach relationship as essential for your child’s success. Here’s how you can be involved:

1.Positive Reinforcement: Encourage your child to stay motivated and focused. Celebrate their progress, both big and small, to boost their confidence.

2. Stay Engaged: We encourage regular communication with our coaches to ensure you’re updated on your child’s development. We also hold parent meetings to keep you informed about upcoming events and goals.

3. Focus on Health and Wellness: A balanced diet, adequate rest, and a commitment to overall fitness are crucial for your child’s success. We also offer fitness programs to help parents support their child’s physical health.

4. Be Involved: Attend academy events and matches to show your support and encourage your child in their football journey.
